By car

Nearest car park
Near the main entrance. Spaces for disabled badge holders are at various points.

Car parking charges

  • Up to 15 minutes: free (pay on foot car parks only)
  • Up to 1 hour: £2.50
  • Up to 2 hours: £3.50
  • Up to 3 hours: £4.00
  • Up to 5 hours: £4.50
  • Up to 24 hours: £5.00

For regular visitors, season tickets are available to give unlimited parking.

  • 3 day £9 + returnable £5 deposit (ideal if you visit your baby on more than one occasion each day and your baby is only in for a short period)
  • 7 day £18 + returnable £5 deposit (ideal if you visit your baby on more than one occasion each day)
  • 3 months £42 + returnable £5 deposit (ideal if your baby is going to be in hospital for more than 3 weeks)
  • One Time Tickets purchased in packs of 4 £10. Each ticket is valid for one visit of any duration (ideal if you visit your baby on one occasion once a day)

The Treatment Centre help desk provides advice about the cheapest parking charges.

Numbers of visitors

Restrictions

Hygiene and infection prevention
Please wash and gel hands before entering the Unit.

What to expect when you arrive

How to access the Unit
Through maternity block. Neonatal Unit is well signposted. Please press the buzzer to enter.

Who to talk to about your baby’s care
The nurse caring for your baby.

Who to talk to if you have any concerns
The nurse in charge, or Jannine Hingley (Manager).

Talking to the medical team
The consultant undertakes a daily ward round.

Ward round
Ward round takes place between 9am and 12, parents are welcome.

Baby rest time
Twice daily, but we try to keep the unit quiet at all times.
