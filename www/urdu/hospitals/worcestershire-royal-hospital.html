Visiting Policies

Parents
Welcome all day.

Siblings
Welcome all day.

Grandparents
Every day 15:00 to 16:00 and 17:00 to 18:00.

Family and friends
Weekends 1500 to 1600 and 1700 to 1800.

Numbers of visitors
1 visitor and 1 parent to each cot.

Restrictions
Only the baby’s brother and sisters may visit, no other children under the age of 16.

Hygiene and infection prevention
Gel hands on entrance to unit and wash hands at basin in each room. Gel hands before touching baby and when leaving the unit.

Neonatal unit facilities

Parent accommodation
Ben Bennett Suite - 2 bedrooms and a sitting room with TV, fridge and microwave at no cost. Parents usually stay here for a few days before baby is discharged home. If your baby is very poorly you may use these facilities.

Parent facilities
Lockers for parents’ belongings and portable DVD players are available. There are no facilities for refreshments on the Unit. Please ask staff as if the parent’s suite is unoccupied you may be able to use the facilities there.

Mobile phone use
Please do not use your phone on the Neonatal Unit.

Telephone and computer access
In an emergency parents are able to use the Unit telephone.

Breastfeeding facilities and support
Breast feeding room available with access to breast pumps, support from nurses and nursery nurses. Additional support is available from the maternity breastfeeding advisor. Free loan of breast pump.

Facilities for siblings
None.

Parent support group
None at present.
